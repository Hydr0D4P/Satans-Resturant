About us

The Project

This website is a project and not a real resturant/cafe, its simply a mock up in order to practice using html, css, and the use of github. If you stumble opon this website, know it was made in september 2021. I thought it would be funny if there was a Cafe that just so happened to have satanic imagery. I am not Satanic, and just enjoyed the idea of the contrast between Satanism, and a cafe.

Accessability

If you were to take a look at the html and css for this page, I've tried to make things as accessable as I can. There are definitely things that could be improved, but I've done what I could. I picked a background that has colours that contrast pretty well against white, and where I can, there is a transparent background around the text. There is also an outline class that is used where the background is either simply not enough or where a background could not be used. In an earlier itteration of this page the navigation bar used to have 6 or 7 different pages to jump to. I found that to be incredibly confusing and it doesn't really help the page in any way. I wanted to have the nav bar follow the scroll, and potentially even have drop down menus, but I settled for something simpler to prevent things from breaking.
